US Markets Decline as Inflation Concerns Linger

TL;DR

The US markets declined on Tuesday, with the S&P 500 falling by -0.45% to 7,503.85 and the Nasdaq 100 dropping by -1.77% to 29,173.02. The Dow Jones also fell by -0.25% to 52,925.15.

The decline was driven by concerns over inflation, with the 10Y Yield rising by +0.98% to 4.53 and WTI Crude increasing by +2.57% to 72.25. The VIX also surged by +3.60% to 16.13.
