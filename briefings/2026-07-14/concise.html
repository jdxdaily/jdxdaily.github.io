US Markets Slide as VIX Surges 14.17% and Oil Prices Jump 11.41%

A 5-minute read of yesterday's US session and today's catalysts — what to watch before pre-market opens at 21:30 HKT.

TL;DR

The US markets experienced a decline in the previous session, with the S&P 500 falling -0.79% to 7,515.34 and the Nasdaq 100 dropping -1.88% to 29,264.10. The VIX surged 14.17% to 17.16, indicating increased market volatility.

Tech stocks were among the biggest losers, with NVDA down -3.52% to 203.53 and TSLA falling -3.19% to 394.76. In contrast, energy stocks gained, with XLE rising 3.01% to 56.74.
