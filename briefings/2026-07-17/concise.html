TL;DR

The US markets ended lower yesterday, with the S&P 500 down -0.51% and the Nasdaq 100 falling -1.62%. The VIX surged 6.76% to 16.73, indicating increased market volatility.

Tech stocks were among the biggest losers, with NVDA down -2.40% and GOOGL falling -4.44%. In contrast, consumer staples and healthcare stocks outperformed, with XLP up 2.80% and XLV rising 2.22%.
