Russell 2000 Leads Gains as US Stocks Rebound

A wider read of yesterday's session — sector rotation, pre-market action, watchlist scan, and what to watch into the US open at 21:30 HKT.

10-minute read · Topics: Macro · Geopolitics · Earnings · Sectors · Watchlist Download PDF

TL;DR

The Russell 2000 led gains among US indices, rising 0.71% to 3,007.86. This move was driven by a rebound in small-cap stocks, which had been under pressure in recent sessions. Below the surface, the VIX fell -2.54% to 18.41, indicating a decrease in market volatility.

Despite the lack of data on major indices, the move in the Russell 2000 and the VIX suggests that investors are becoming more risk-tolerant. This is also reflected in the price of gold, which rose +1.20% to 4,078.70. The DXY, on the other hand, fell -0.18% to 101.43, indicating a slight decrease in the value of the US dollar.
