Dow Surges +1.14% as Energy and Financials Lead the Charge

A 5-minute read of yesterday's US session and today's catalysts — what to watch before pre-market opens at 21:30 HKT.

TL;DR

The US market saw a mixed session, with the Dow Jones surging +1.14% to 52,900.07 and the S&P 500 closing flat at 7,483.24. The Nasdaq 100, however, fell -1.61% to 29,329.21. Energy and financial stocks led the charge, with the XLE and XLF sector ETFs rising +0.78% and +1.53%, respectively.

Tech stocks, on the other hand, struggled, with NVDA and TSLA falling -1.39% and -7.49%, respectively. The VIX fell -2.11% to 15.81, indicating a decrease in market volatility.
