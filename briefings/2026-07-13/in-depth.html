TL;DR & Editorial Take

The US market saw a positive session with the S&P 500 rising +0.42% to 7,575.39. The Nasdaq 100 also gained +0.33% to 29,825.11. Key drivers included energy and tech stocks, with NVDA surging +4.03% and META jumping +5.97%.

Our editorial take is that while the market's momentum is positive, investors should remain cautious due to ongoing macroeconomic uncertainties. Key points to watch include:

  • The 10Y Yield rose +0.66% to 4.57, indicating potential inflation concerns.
  • WTI Crude prices surged +4.13% to 74.36, which could impact energy stocks.
  • The VIX fell -5.11% to 15.03, suggesting reduced market volatility.

Overall, the market's direction will depend on the interplay between these factors and the upcoming earnings season.
