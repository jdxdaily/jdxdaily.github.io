TL;DR

The US market saw a positive session with the S&P 500 closing at 7,575.39, up +0.42%. The Nasdaq 100 and Dow Jones also rose, with gains of +0.33% and +0.29% respectively. The Russell 2000 was the only major index to decline, falling -0.49%.
