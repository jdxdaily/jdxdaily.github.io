US Markets End Mixed as Tech Stocks Weigh on Nasdaq

A wider read of yesterday's session — sector rotation, pre-market action, watchlist scan, and what to watch into the US open at 21:30 HKT.

TL;DR

The US markets ended the previous session on a mixed note, with the S&P 500 closing at 7,354.02, down -0.05%, while the Nasdaq 100 fell -1.09% to 29,118.24. The Dow Jones also declined, losing -0.09% to close at 51,876.11. However, the Russell 2000 managed to eke out a gain of +0.07% to 3,010.08.

Below the surface, the VIX fell -2.54% to 18.41, indicating a decrease in market volatility. The 10Y Yield also declined, losing -0.46% to 4.37. In the commodity space, WTI Crude dropped -3.74% to 69.23, while Gold rose +1.63% to 4,096.30.
