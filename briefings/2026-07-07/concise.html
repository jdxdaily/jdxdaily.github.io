US Markets Rise as Tech Stocks Lead the Charge

A 5-minute read of yesterday's US session and today's catalysts — what to watch before pre-market opens at 21:30 HKT.

TL;DR

The US markets rose on Monday, with the Nasdaq 100 leading the charge, up +1.26% to 29,697.87. The S&P 500 and Dow Jones also gained, rising +0.72% to 7,537.43 and +0.29% to 53,055.91, respectively.

Tech stocks were the main drivers of the rally, with TSLA surging +6.69% to 419.77 and META rising +2.98% to 600.29. The VIX fell -3.59% to 15.57, indicating a decrease in market volatility.
