US Stocks Rise as Tech Leads with +0.70% Nasdaq Gain

TL;DR

The US market saw a positive session with the Nasdaq 100 leading the charge, closing at 30,545.89 with a +0.70% gain. The S&P 500 and Dow Jones also ended in the green, at 7,609.78 and 51,097.29 respectively. The VIX rose +3.20% to 15.81, indicating increased market volatility.

Tech stocks were the main drivers of the rally, with NVDA surging +5.81% and MSFT gaining +3.00%. In contrast, the Russell 2000 declined -0.26% to 2,911.81, and META plummeted -4.39% to 604.72.
