TL;DR & Editorial Take

The US markets experienced a significant decline in the previous session, with the S&P 500 dropping -1.62% and the Nasdaq 100 falling -1.98%. This downturn was largely driven by intensifying inflation concerns, which have led to increased expectations of interest rate hikes. Key points to consider include:

  • The VIX surged +11.83% to 22.22, indicating heightened market volatility.
  • The 10Y yield rose +0.31% to 4.54, reflecting growing inflation worries.
  • WTI crude oil prices jumped +4.63% to 92.28, further fueling inflation concerns.

As we move forward, it is essential to closely monitor these key indicators and their impact on the market. The current environment suggests that investors should be prepared for potential further declines and increased volatility.

In the coming days, we will be keeping a close eye on the S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100 for any signs of stabilization or potential rebounds. Additionally, we will be monitoring the VIX and 10Y yield for any changes in market sentiment.
