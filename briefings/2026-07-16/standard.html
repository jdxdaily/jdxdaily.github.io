TL;DR

The S&P 500 rose 0.38% to 7,572.40 as tech stocks rebounded, led by AAPL and MSFT. The yield curve steepened, with the 10Y yield falling -0.87% to 4.55. Below the surface, sector rotation favored consumer discretionary and communication services, while the VIX fell -5.03% to 15.67.

Looking ahead, the focus shifts to the US open, where futures are pointing to a mixed start. The Dow Jones is up 52 points, while the Nasdaq 100 is down 10 points. In Asia, the Nikkei 225 rose 0.5%, while the Hang Seng Index fell -0.2%. The euro is up 0.2% against the dollar, while gold is down -0.42% to 4,044.00.
