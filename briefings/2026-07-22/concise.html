US Markets Rally as Tech Stocks Lead the Charge

A 5-minute read of yesterday's US session and today's catalysts — what to watch before pre-market opens at 21:30 HKT.

2-3 page read · Topics: Macro · Geopolitics · Earnings Download PDF

TL;DR

The US markets rallied yesterday, with the Nasdaq 100 leading the charge with a +1.93% gain. The S&P 500 and Dow Jones also posted gains, rising +0.89% and +0.74% respectively.

Tech stocks were the main drivers of the rally, with NVDA and TSLA posting gains of +1.97% and +2.53% respectively. The VIX fell -8.58% to 17.05, indicating a decrease in market volatility.
