TL;DR & Editorial Take

The US market saw a significant rally yesterday, led by the tech sector with the NVDA stock rising by +1.97%. The Nasdaq 100 index closed +1.93% higher, while the S&P 500 and Dow Jones indices also saw gains of +0.89% and +0.74%, respectively.
