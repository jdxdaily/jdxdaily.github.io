TL;DR & Editorial Take

The US market rallied on the back of strong tech performance, with NVDA and TSLA leading the charge. The S&P 500 closed at 7,440.43, up +1.18% for the day. The Nasdaq 100 outperformed, closing at 29,774.75, up +2.25%.

The rally was driven by a combination of factors, including a decline in the 10Y Yield to 4.37 and a drop in the VIX to 17.65. The market is now looking ahead to the next Fed meeting, with 52.1% of traders expecting a rate cut.
