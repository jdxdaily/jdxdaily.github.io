TL;DR

The US market saw a significant rally in the previous session, led by tech stocks, with the Nasdaq 100 gaining +2.25% to close at 29,774.75. The S&P 500 and Dow Jones also ended in the green, with gains of +1.18% and +0.59%, respectively.
