US Stocks Rally as Tech Leads with +2.25% Nasdaq Gain

A wider read of yesterday's session — sector rotation, pre-market action, watchlist scan, and what to watch into the US open at 21:30 HKT.

10-minute read · Topics: Macro · Geopolitics · Earnings · Sectors · Watchlist Download PDF

TL;DR

The US market rallied yesterday, led by the tech sector, with the Nasdaq 100 gaining +2.25%. The S&P 500 also rose, closing at 7,440.43, up +1.18%. Below the surface, the VIX fell -4.13% to 17.65, indicating decreased volatility.

The rally was driven by strong gains in large-cap tech stocks, including GOOGL, which rose +4.82%, and TSLA, which gained +8.46%. The Dow Jones also rose, but at a slower pace, closing up +0.59% at 52,182.74.
