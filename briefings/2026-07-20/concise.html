TL;DR

The US market experienced a decline in the previous session, with the S&P 500, Dow Jones, and Nasdaq 100 all posting losses. The VIX index surged 12.19%, indicating increased market volatility. The 10Y yield fell 0.61%, while WTI crude oil rose 1.50%.
