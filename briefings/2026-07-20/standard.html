TL;DR

The US stock market extended its losses on Thursday, with the S&P 500 falling -1.01% to 7,457.69 and the Nasdaq 100 dropping -1.49% to 28,592.66. The Dow Jones declined -0.77% to 52,146.42, while the Russell 2000 edged down -0.42% to 2,962.22. The VIX jumped +12.19% to 18.77, indicating increased market volatility.

Below the surface, the 10Y Yield fell -0.61% to 4.54, while WTI Crude rose +1.50% to 83.73. Gold edged up +0.33% to 4,026.10, and the DXY slipped -0.04% to 100.71. BTC fell -0.14% to 64,703.87, while ETH gained +0.69% to 1,874.15.
