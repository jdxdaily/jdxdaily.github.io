TL;DR & Editorial Take

The US market experienced a significant downturn, with the S&P 500 dropping 2.64% and the Nasdaq 100 plummeting 4.77%. This decline was accompanied by a surge in the VIX, which rose 39.68% to 21.51. The 10Y yield also increased, jumping 1.32% to 4.54.

The editorial take is that the market is experiencing a period of high volatility, driven by concerns over interest rates and the overall health of the economy. The significant increase in the VIX and the jump in yields suggest that investors are becoming increasingly risk-averse.

The key points to watch are:

  • The S&P 500's ability to hold above the 7,300 level
  • The Nasdaq 100's potential to bounce back from its recent decline
  • The impact of the rising 10Y yield on the overall market
