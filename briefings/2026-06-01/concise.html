US Stocks End Mixed as Tech Leads Gains

A 5-minute read of yesterday's US session and today's catalysts — what to watch before pre-market opens at 21:30 HKT.

TL;DR

The US stock market ended the previous session mixed, with the S&P 500 closing at 7,580.06, up +0.22%, while the Nasdaq 100 outperformed with a +0.36% gain to 30,333.18. The Dow Jones led the major indices, rising +0.72% to 51,032.46.

Tech stocks were among the top performers, with MSFT surging +5.45% to 450.24 and AVGO gaining +4.73% to 446.77. In contrast, the Russell 2000 fell -0.59% to 2,919.34, and the VIX declined -2.67% to 15.32.
