TL;DR & Editorial Take

The US market rally continued with the Nasdaq 100 leading the charge, up +1.68%. The S&P 500 and Dow Jones also posted gains, +0.79% and +0.26% respectively. Key drivers included strong tech performance and a slight increase in oil prices.

The current market trend is supported by a combination of factors including positive earnings reports, low unemployment rates, and a stable economic environment. However, investors should remain cautious due to potential risks such as inflation, interest rate changes, and global geopolitical tensions.

Key points to watch include:

  • Technicals: The S&P 500 is approaching its 50-day moving average, which could act as a resistance level.
  • Sector performance: The tech sector, led by NVDA and MSFT, is driving the market rally.
  • Economic indicators: The upcoming jobs report and inflation data will be crucial in determining the market's direction.
