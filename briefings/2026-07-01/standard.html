US Stocks Rise as Tech Sector Leads with +2.76% Gain in XLK

A wider read of yesterday's session — sector rotation, pre-market action, watchlist scan, and what to watch into the US open at 21:30 HKT.

TL;DR

The US market saw a significant gain in the tech sector, with the XLK rising by +2.76%. This was driven by strong performances from large-cap tech stocks such as AAPL, MSFT, and NVDA. The S&P 500 also saw a gain of +0.79%, reaching a level of 7,499.36.

Below the surface, the market is seeing a rotation into tech and away from other sectors such as financials and energy. The XLF fell by -0.20%, while the XLE fell by -0.88%. This rotation is likely driven by investor expectations of a continued strong performance from the tech sector, as well as concerns about the impact of rising interest rates on other sectors.
