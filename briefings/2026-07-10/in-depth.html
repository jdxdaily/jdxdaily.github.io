TL;DR & Editorial Take

The US stock market rebounded on Thursday, led by a strong performance from the tech sector. The Nasdaq 100 rose +1.62%, outpacing the +0.81% gain in the S&P 500. The Dow Jones lagged, up +0.27%.

The rally was driven by a combination of factors, including a decline in bond yields and a rebound in semiconductor stocks. NVDA fell -0.66%, but other chipmakers such as AVGO rose +3.20%.

Key takeaways from the session include:

  • The VIX fell -6.27% to 15.84, indicating a decrease in market volatility.
  • The 10Y yield fell -0.66% to 4.54, weighing on the Dollar index.
  • BTC rose +2.90% to 64,064.50, outpacing other major cryptocurrencies.
