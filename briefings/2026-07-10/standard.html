US Stocks Extend Gains as Tech Leads the Charge

A wider read of yesterday's session — sector rotation, pre-market action, watchlist scan, and what to watch into the US open at 21:30 HKT.

10-minute read · Topics: Macro · Geopolitics · Earnings · Sectors · Watchlist Download PDF

TL;DR

The US market extended its gains yesterday, with the Nasdaq 100 leading the charge with a +1.62% move. The S&P 500 also closed higher, up +0.81%. Below the surface, the VIX fell -6.27%, indicating a decrease in market volatility.

The rally was led by the tech sector, with META and TSLA posting significant gains of +4.70% and +3.17%, respectively. The sector's strength was also reflected in the XLK ETF, which rose +2.18%.
