TL;DR

The US market saw a mixed performance yesterday, with the Dow Jones rising +0.29% to 51,712.71, while the S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100 declined -0.37% and -0.19% respectively. The Russell 2000 outperformed, gaining +0.83% to 3,004.40. Below the surface, the VIX jumped +5.37% to 17.28, indicating increased volatility.

The 10-year yield rose +1.30% to 4.51, while WTI Crude fell -3.36% to 74.03. The DXY edged up +0.17% to 101.02. In the cryptocurrency space, BTC and ETH rose +1.38% and +1.55% respectively.
