TL;DR

The US market ended the session mixed, with the Dow Jones being the sole gainer among the major indices, closing at 51,712.71 with a +0.29% increase. The S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100 closed lower, at 7,472.79 and 30,347.08 respectively, with declines of -0.37% and -0.19%. The VIX spiked +5.37% to 17.28, indicating increased volatility.

Today's focus will be on the earnings calendar, with several key companies reporting. The macroeconomic calendar is relatively light, with the main event being the 10Y Yield reaching 4.51, a +1.30% increase.
