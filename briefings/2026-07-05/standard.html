Dow Surges +1.14% as Energy and Financials Lead, While Tech Suffers

A wider read of yesterday's session — sector rotation, pre-market action, watchlist scan, and what to watch into the US open at 21:30 HKT.

10-minute read · Topics: Macro · Geopolitics · Earnings · Sectors · Watchlist Download PDF

TL;DR

The Dow Jones index rose +1.14% to 52,900.07, driven by gains in energy and financial stocks, while the Nasdaq 100 fell -1.61% to 29,329.21 due to a decline in tech stocks. The VIX volatility index decreased -2.65% to 16.15, indicating a decrease in market volatility.

Below the surface, sector rotation was a key theme, with investors moving out of tech and into more defensive sectors such as consumer staples and healthcare. The XLP consumer staples ETF rose +2.03% to 84.99, while the XLV healthcare ETF gained +2.63% to 163.74.
