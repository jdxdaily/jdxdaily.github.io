TL;DR & Editorial Take

The US market saw a positive session with the S&P 500 closing at 7,575.39, up +0.42%. The Nasdaq 100 outperformed, rising +0.33% to 29,825.11. Key movers included NVDA, up +4.03%, and META, up +5.97%.

Our editorial take is that the market is showing resilience despite concerns over inflation and interest rates. The strong performance of tech stocks is a significant driver of this trend.

Key points to watch:

  • The 10Y Yield rose to 4.57, up +0.66%, indicating a potential shift in interest rates.
  • The VIX fell to 15.03, down -5.11%, suggesting decreased volatility.
  • BTC and ETH saw significant gains, up +1.37% and +2.89% respectively.
