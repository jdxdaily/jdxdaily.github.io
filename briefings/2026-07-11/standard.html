TL;DR

The US stock market rose yesterday, led by the tech sector, with NVDA surging +4.03% after a strong earnings report. The S&P 500 closed at 7,575.39, up +0.42%, while the Nasdaq 100 gained +0.33% to 29,825.11. Below the surface, sector rotation and earnings reports will be key to watch in the coming days.

Investors will also be watching the macroeconomic data, including the inflation report, to gauge the Federal Reserve's next move. The yield curve and interest rates will also be closely monitored, as they have been a key driver of market sentiment in recent weeks.
