TL;DR

The US market saw a significant rally in the last session, with the Nasdaq 100 leading the charge with a +2.48% gain. This surge was largely driven by the tech sector, with NVDA and AMZN posting notable gains of +2.95% and +2.90%, respectively.

Today, investors will be watching for further developments in the tech sector, as well as any updates on the macroeconomic front. The 10Y Yield saw a slight decrease to 4.45, while WTI Crude prices rose by +1.19% to 77.51.
