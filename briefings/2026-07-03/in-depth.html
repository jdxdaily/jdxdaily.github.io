TL;DR & Editorial Take

The US market saw a mixed session yesterday, with the Dow Jones surging +1.14% and the S&P 500 holding steady at 7,483.24, while the Nasdaq 100 dropped -1.61% to 29,329.21. The VIX fell -2.65% to 16.15, indicating a decrease in market volatility.

Key takeaways from the session include:

  • The Dow's surge was led by gains in JPM and GS, which rose +0.12% and +0.14%, respectively.
  • The Nasdaq's drop was driven by declines in NVDA and TSLA, which fell -1.39% and -7.49%, respectively.
  • The S&P 500's stability was supported by gains in AAPL and MSFT, which rose +4.84% and +1.62%, respectively.
