Nasdaq 100 Leads Gains as Tech Stocks Rebound

A wider read of yesterday's session — sector rotation, pre-market action, watchlist scan, and what to watch into the US open at 21:30 HKT.

10-minute read · Topics: Macro · Geopolitics · Earnings · Sectors · Watchlist Download PDF

TL;DR

The Nasdaq 100 led gains among major US indices, rising 1.10% as tech stocks rebounded. The S&P 500 and Dow Jones also closed higher, up 0.38% and 0.02% respectively. Beneath the surface, the VIX fell 3.85% to 16.50, while the 10Y yield declined 0.52% to 4.59.

A closer look at sector rotation reveals that tech and financials led the charge, with the XLK and XLF sector ETFs rising 1.29% and 0.20% respectively. The energy sector also saw gains, with WTI crude oil rising 2.41% to 80.02. Meanwhile, the DXY fell 0.45% to 100.83, while gold rose 1.02% to 4,037.60.
