US Markets Reel as Tech Selloff Intensifies, NVDA and TSLA Lead Declines

A wider read of yesterday's session — sector rotation, pre-market action, watchlist scan, and what to watch into the US open at 21:30 HKT.

TL;DR

The US market sell-off deepened yesterday, with the Nasdaq 100 plummeting -3.29% and the S&P 500 falling -1.44%. Beneath the surface, the VIX surged +12.79% to 19.49, indicating heightened volatility. The 10Y Yield also jumped +0.94% to 4.49, further pressuring equity valuations.

Notable movers included NVDA, down -4.13%, and TSLA, which plummeted -5.79%. The sector rotation saw defensive plays like XLP and XLV outperform, rising +1.87% and +1.41%, respectively. As we head into the US open, keep an eye on AAPL and MSFT, which reported relatively modest declines of -0.91% and +1.80%, respectively.
