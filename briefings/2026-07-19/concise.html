US Stocks Decline as VIX Surges 12.19% and Oil Prices Jump 3.58%

A 5-minute read of yesterday's US session and today's catalysts — what to watch before pre-market opens at 21:30 HKT.

TL;DR

The US stock market experienced a decline in the previous session, with the S&P 500 dropping -1.01% to 7,457.69 and the Nasdaq 100 falling -1.49% to 28,592.66. The VIX surged 12.19% to 18.77, indicating increased market volatility.

The decline in stocks was accompanied by a rise in oil prices, with WTI Crude jumping 3.58% to 81.78. The 10Y Yield fell -0.61% to 4.54, while Gold rose 0.83% to 4,018.80.
