US Markets Reel as S&P 500 Drops -1.01% and VIX Surges +12.19%

TL;DR

The S&P 500 plummeted -1.01% to 7,457.69, while the Nasdaq 100 dropped -1.49% to 28,592.66. The VIX surged +12.19% to 18.77, indicating increased market volatility. Below the surface, the 10Y Yield fell -0.61% to 4.54, and WTI Crude rose +3.58% to 81.78.

Notable large-cap movers included MSFT down -1.82%, NVDA down -2.21%, and AAPL up +0.14%. The XLE sector ETF rose +1.16%, while the XLY sector ETF fell -1.62%.
