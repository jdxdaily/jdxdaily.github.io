US Stocks Extend Losses as S&P 500 Falls -1.21% Amid Rising Yields

A wider read of yesterday's session — sector rotation, pre-market action, watchlist scan, and what to watch into the US open at 21:30 HKT.

10-minute read · Topics: Macro · Geopolitics · Earnings · Sectors · Watchlist Download PDF

TL;DR

The S&P 500 fell -1.21% to 7,408.30 as the 10Y Yield rose +0.99% to 4.70, weighing on growth stocks. Below the surface, the XLI sector ETF gained +1.73% as investors rotated into value plays.

The Nasdaq 100 underperformed, falling -1.87% to 28,454.81 as tech giants NVDA, MSFT, and GOOGL all declined. The VIX jumped +12.38% to 18.70, indicating increased market volatility.
