Dow Surges +1.14% as Rates and Energy Prices Fuel Rally

A wider read of yesterday's session — sector rotation, pre-market action, watchlist scan, and what to watch into the US open at 21:30 HKT.

10-minute read · Topics: Macro · Geopolitics · Earnings · Sectors · Watchlist Download PDF

TL;DR

The Dow Jones surged +1.14% to 52,900.07 as higher rates and energy prices fueled a rally in financial and energy stocks. The XLF and XLE sector ETFs led the charge, rising +1.53% and +0.78% respectively. Meanwhile, the Nasdaq 100 fell -1.61% to 29,329.21 as tech stocks struggled.

Below the surface, the 10Y Yield rose +0.22% to 4.49, while gold prices jumped +1.40% to 4,170.40. The DXY index edged up +0.10% to 100.96, while the VIX fell -2.65% to 16.15.
