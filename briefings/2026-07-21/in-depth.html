TL;DR & Editorial Take

The US market saw a mixed session on July 20, with the SPY closing down -0.19% and the QQQ up +0.04%. The DOW was the biggest loser, down -0.59%. Key takeaways include:

  • The NVDA and MSFT led the tech sector higher, with gains of +0.23% and +2.15% respectively.
  • The VIX closed down -0.64% at 18.65, indicating a decrease in market volatility.
  • The 10-year yield was up +1.26% at 4.60, its highest level in weeks.

The market's mixed performance can be attributed to the ongoing tug-of-war between inflation concerns and growth expectations. As we head into the week ahead, investors will be closely watching the Fed's rate decision and the latest earnings reports.

The SPY is currently trading at 443.28, with a 200-day moving average of 434.12. The QQQ is trading at 358.42, with a 200-day moving average of 346.51.
