TL;DR

The US stock market ended the previous session on a mixed note, with the Nasdaq 100 gaining +0.04% and the S&P 500 losing -0.19%. Beneath the surface, the +1.26% jump in the 10Y Yield suggests a shift in investor sentiment towards a more hawkish Fed stance. Additionally, the +1.34% increase in BTC and +2.84% in ETH indicates a resurgence of interest in cryptocurrencies.

The sector rotation also revealed interesting trends, with XLK (tech) and XLE (energy) being the top gainers, while XLV (healthcare) and XLB (materials) were the biggest losers. This rotation could be a sign of investors seeking growth in tech and energy, while becoming cautious about the healthcare and materials sectors.
