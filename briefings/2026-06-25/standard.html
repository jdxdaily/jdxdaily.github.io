TL;DR

Yesterday's US session saw a mixed close with the Dow Jones index bucking the trend, ending +0.35% higher at 51,848.90. The S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100 closed -0.10% and -0.43% respectively. Below the surface, sector rotation favored the XLI and XLY ETFs, which rose +1.16% and +1.15%.

A closer look at the numbers reveals that the VIX fell -4.41% to 18.63, indicating a decrease in market volatility. The 10Y Yield also decreased by -1.10% to 4.40. These moves suggest that investors are becoming less risk-averse, which could have implications for the broader market.
