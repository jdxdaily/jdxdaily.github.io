TL;DR

The Nasdaq 100 closed at 29,825.11, up +0.33%, driven by strong performances from NVDA and META. The Dow Jones also rose, gaining +0.29% to close at 52,637.01. However, the Russell 2000 declined -0.49% to 2,977.81, and the VIX dropped -5.11% to 15.03.

Below the surface, sector rotation was notable, with XLC and XLP leading the charge, up +1.02% and +1.11% respectively. The 10Y Yield rose +0.66% to 4.57, while WTI Crude fell -0.93% to 71.41. Gold declined -0.41% to 4,113.70, and the DXY inched up +0.03% to 100.97.
