What Could Break the Tape

BULLISH TRIGGERS
  • Strong earnings reports from major companies
  • Positive economic data, such as low unemployment and high GDP growth
  • Central banks maintaining accommodative monetary policies
  • Geopolitical tensions easing, leading to increased investor confidence
BEARISH TRIGGERS
  • Disappointing earnings reports from major companies
  • Negative economic data, such as high unemployment and low GDP growth
  • Central banks tightening monetary policies, leading to higher interest rates
  • Geopolitical tensions escalating, leading to decreased investor confidence
