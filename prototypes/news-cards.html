News Card — Design Prototypes

Four directions. Pick one (or a hybrid) and I'll wire it into the live site.

OPTION 1 Magazine — bold filled badge, top-left

Solid color badge sits on the image's top-left like a magazine cover. Category tucks into bottom-right of the image. Image flows straight into the body with no colored strip. Tickers + source live in a quiet metadata row at the bottom.
