US Stocks Rise as Tech Leads the Charge, Nasdaq 100 Up +1.93%

The US market saw a broad-based rally yesterday, with the Nasdaq 100 leading the charge, up +1.93%. The S&P 500 and Dow Jones also closed higher, up +0.89% and +0.74%, respectively. Beneath the surface, the rally was driven by a combination of factors, including a decline in volatility, with the VIX down -8.58%, and a surge in tech stocks, led by NVDA, up +1.97%.
